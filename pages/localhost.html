About Our Local Host

O.P. Jindal Global University (JGU), a private deemed-to-be university located in Sonipat, Haryana, near New Delhi, was established in 2009 as a philanthropic initiative by its founding chancellor, Naveen Jindal, in memory of O.P. Jindal.

Recognized as an Institute of Eminence by the UGC in 2020, JGU offers 45 multidisciplinary programs across law, liberal arts, life sciences, and business. Guided by its mission to develop future leaders and professionals, JGU emphasizes experiential learning, global collaborations, and innovation to prepare students for real-world challenges.

With a commitment to academic freedom, ethical integrity, social responsibility, and inclusive access, JGU fosters a holistic educational environment. It strives to cultivate industry-ready talent, promote sustainable development, and empower students to become globally competent leaders who drive societal transformation.
